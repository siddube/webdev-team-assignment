Directions

From Route 55

  1. Exit Route 55 at Galaxy Junction
  2. At traffic light, turn left
  3. Continue straight for 2 miles
  4. Turn right on Gravity Avenue
  5. Theme Park will be on your right

Using the Luna Link Train

  1. Board train L northbound from your place of origin
  2. Get off at stop 'Space Park'
  3. Exit Train Station building at lower level
  4. Entrace to park will be right ahead of you

Food
  • There are 5 kiosks spread around the park
  • There is a Vegan Resturant in the park 'Aint no animals in space"
  • Visitors are welcome to bring their own snacks and leave them in the lockers at the entrance to the park

Accessibility
  • The Burger Bar resturant and Milkyway Takeout have wheel chair accesible entrances
  • All signage is in braille
  • Guide dogs welcome on a leash with a muzzle
